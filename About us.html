    Our Company Name is:- DOITAME PRODUCTION
Doitamie Production is organized to inspire and develop young minds to enjoy the exploration of their creative side.Membership shall consist of employees and students of the company. The date of the regular annual meeting shall be set by the Board of Executives, who shall also set the time and place. Special meetings may be called by the Chief Executive Officer or the Executive Committee.
